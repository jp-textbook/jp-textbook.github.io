Japanese Textbook LOD

Japanese Textbook Linked Open Data (LOD) is a dataset which includes bibliographic information for textbooks published up to now since the 7th curriculum guideline enforced in 1992, as well as the related information such as the subject areas, the subjects, and the curriculum guidelines.

https://w3id.org/jp-textbook/
